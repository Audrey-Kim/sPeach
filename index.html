sPeach

Words become as sweet as Peaches.

Our Goal

To empower more people to speak in public.

Speaking is the most essential form of communication amongst humans. Without it, ideas would not be spread, and a person would be trapped in a lonely world. What if a person had the ability to speak, but could not convey their intentions as they wish? Public speaking is a vital skill that everyone needs. Unfortunately, not everyone on the planet is able to get mentorship for this crucial gift. This our mission. We want to make an app that provide everyone in the world this amazing opportunity: to develop advanced public speaking skills. Our main audience are third-world countries where these teachings are not provided.

  • Help women share their stories in the #MeToo Movement.
  • The underprivileged can speak up for their rights and what their deserve.
  • Activists can understand how to properly express their beliefs.

The App

1

Recite your speech

Speak loud and clear into the mic. Your speech will be recorded, and interpreted by the app.

  • Captures anyone's voice.
  • Easily access speech audio later.
  • When finished, view results.
2

View Results

As soon as you finish your speech, the app will analyze the audio. Next, it will suggest any improvements it thinks you can make. sPeach will state the words you repeated the most, the number of pauses, and explain techniques for improving tone.

  • Checks for all sorts of mistakes in speech.
  • Reports positive and negative feedback.
  • Gives grade based on feedback, as well as improvement.
3

Share

Coming soon. When you finish your speech, you may share it with friends and family, as well as live trainers, who can give further feedback.

  • Seamlessly link to your Facebook, Twitter, and other social media.
  • Challenge your friends.
  • Take our survey to help us improve!
